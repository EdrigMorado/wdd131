What is Restless Minds?

Restless Minds is a cybersecurity and digital protection company focused on helping small businesses and microbusinesses access practical cybersecurity services without unnecessary complexity. Many smaller organizations need protection, but they do not always have the budget, knowledge, or technical staff required to manage digital risks on their own.

The project was born from the desire to help others stay safer in a world where digital threats are increasingly common. The goal is to provide clear guidance, practical security audits, camera and access control support, and educational resources that help people understand how to protect their systems, accounts, and physical spaces.
