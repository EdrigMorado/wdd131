Cybersecurity should not be reserved only for large companies.

Restless Minds was created to help smaller organizations and everyday users access practical digital protection. Many small businesses, microbusinesses, and private communities face real security risks, but they often lack the technical support needed to identify and reduce them.

The goal is to provide clear guidance, practical security services, and educational write-ups that help people understand threats, protect their systems, and improve their security mindset.
